# Employee-Performance-Analysis

This project analyzes employee performance data to identify key factors influencing KPI achievement and support HR decision-making.

## Overview
- Dataset: 17,415 employees  
- KPI achievement rate: ~36%  
- Tools: SQL, Python, Power BI  

## Key Insights
- Previous year rating is the strongest predictor of KPI performance  
- Referral recruitment channel yields higher-performing employees  
- Training quality improves performance, while excessive training reduces efficiency  
- Department and tenure significantly impact employee performance  

## Business Impact
The analysis supports HR teams in improving recruitment strategy, optimizing training programs, and enhancing workforce performance.
